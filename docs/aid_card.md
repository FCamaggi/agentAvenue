Use the side of the board **without black market spaces**.
No black market cards. Deal each player **4 agent cards**.

Whenever you have less than 4 cards in hand, draw cards until you have 4.

---

**STEP 1: PLAY**

Play **2 agent cards**: 1 face up and 1 face down.
**They must be different!**

**Optional:** Before you play cards, you may discard a card face down to draw a new one (4 times in the entire game).

---

**STEP 2: RECRUIT**

Your opponent recruits 1 agent card of their choice. You recruit the other.

Both players move their meeple by looking at their newly recruited agent. Check how many copies of that agent you have in play: 1 or 2 or 3+. The corresponding icon indicates how far you move.

---

**STEP 3: END**

Check whether any player fulfills a condition:

- **A)** Win by catching the opponent's meeple
- **B)** Win by having 3 _Codebreakers_
- **C)** Lose by having 3 _Daredevils_
